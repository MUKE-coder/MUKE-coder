I'm a self-taught fullstack developer from **Kampala, Uganda** building products, teaching developers, and shipping open source.

- Founder & CEO of **[Desishub Technologies](https://desishub.com)** — building web apps, mobile apps, and AI solutions for African businesses
- Founder of **[Desishub Coding School](https://desishub.com)** — in-person bootcamps training 100+ students
